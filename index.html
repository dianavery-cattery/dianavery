Welcome to DianAvery

DianAvery Cattery is passionate and dedicated to breeding British Shorthair Colourpointed cats. Our hard work, passion and love for the breed helps us to produce healthy, adorable and well-socialised British Shorthair kittens that meet the official breed standard.
We support and provide expert care guidance to pet owners to ensure these unique companions thrive in their lifelong homes.
